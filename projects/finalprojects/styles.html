Carving Techniques

Explore various wood carving techniques and their unique applications:

  • Relief Carving: Carving figures into a flat surface, often used for decorative panels.
  • Whittling: Using a knife to create intricate designs, perfect for smaller projects.
  • Chip Carving: Removing small chips to create detailed patterns for decoration.
