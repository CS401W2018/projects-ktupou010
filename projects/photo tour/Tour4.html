Vava'u

Tonga Nuku'alofa 'Eua Ha'apai Vava'u Niua

Water, water, water; it’s all about the water in the island archipelago of Vava’u. Its warm sheltered waters are also what attract humpback whales every year to mate and calve, while it also means you have incredible visibility for scuba diving, ideal conditions for kayaking and kitesurfing, and oh yeah, the sports fishing here is something else!
