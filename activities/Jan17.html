3. What are special entity characters?

- Special entity characters or it's also known as HTML entities, are used in web pages to show characters that have special meanings or are hard to type. They make sure these characters appear correctly in web browsers.

4. What are phrase tags?

5. What is the difference between OL and UL Tags?

- The difference between OL and UL is that OL or known as Order List are tag creates a list with numbered items. Each item is marked with a number or letter to show its position in the list and UL or known as Unorder List are tag creates a list with bullet points instead of numbers for each item.
