What are the positives and negatives of hosting your videos on YouTube?

Positives and Negatives of Hosting Videos on YouTube:

  • Positives: YouTube is a widely accessible platform where your videos can reach a large audience for free. It offers easy monetization opportunities, video analytics, and compatibility across devices.
  • Negatives: Hosting on YouTube may subject your videos to advertisements, copyright policies, and potential loss of control over your content. Also, YouTubes algorithm might limit visibility if your content doesnt align with trending topics.

What is the most useful thing you can do with a transform?

Transforms are powerful for visually enhancing your webpage. You can rotate, scale, skew, or translate elements to create dynamic and engaging designs. For example, you might use transforms to animate objects or change their position for interactive effects.
